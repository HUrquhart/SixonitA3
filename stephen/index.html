About me

Stephen Robinson (Student # s3812498), has two daughters and enjoys roller speed skating when he gets away from work and or study. Stephen’s interest in IT began at an early age. He was always interested in computers from a gaming perspective and running games back then wasn’t as straightforward as it is today. Stephen’s experience in IT includes; 2 years building Desktop/Server systems within a retail computer store. 2 years’ experience providing onsite support to businesses and residential customers through a Gold Coast IT company. 1 year of providing business and consumer onsite support for DELL. 2 years as a District Technician for the Department of Education and Training, QLD. 7 years as an IT Support Officer (current position) for a private educational institution
